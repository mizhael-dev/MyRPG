#### Aesthetic and Tone
Grounded

The world is harsh and unforgiving, with muddy battlefields, crumbling cities, and perilous wildernesses.
Life for most people is bleak, with little respite from toil, war, and oppression.
Moments of Dark Humor

Inject sardonic wit and dark humor to lighten the tone without breaking immersion, reflecting the grim absurdity of life in this world.
Themes of Decay and Renewal

The setting should explore cycles of destruction and rebirth: empires rise and fall, heroes fade into legend, and gods lose their power only to be replaced.

1. **Morally Ambiguous Characters**
   - Characters are rarely purely good or evil. Heroes often have significant flaws, and villains may have understandable motivations. 
   - Actions are driven by self-interest, survival, or pragmatic goals rather than noble ideals.

2. **Dark but humorous Tone**
   - themes of despair, betrayal, and the futility of idealism
   - The world is typically unforgiving, filled with corrupted people
   - darkness is lurking, evil supernatural forces are scheming and evil creatures are present
   - gods play with mortals for fun but overall don't care about them
   - regular people try to go about their lives normally
   - Humor is a way to cope with rather bleak world

3. **Violence and Realism**
   - violence is presented but not very graphic, presented in unromanticized manner
   - don't shy away from the physical and psychological costs of war, conflict, and power struggles

4. **Subversion of Tropes**
   - Traditional fantasy tropes like noble heroes, clear-cut good vs. evil, or guaranteed happy endings are subverted.
   - Success often comes at great cost, and failure is a recurring possibility.

5. **Cynical Worldview**
   - The setting often reflects a world where institutions, systems of power, and societal norms are corrupt or broken.
   - Optimism is rare, and any glimmer of hope is hard-earned and fleeting.

6. **Complex Political and Social Themes**
   - explores the dynamics of power, oppression, and rebellion.
   - It delves into the impact of war, class struggles, and the human cost of ambition.
