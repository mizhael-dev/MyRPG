### **Key Character Profiles**

---

#### **Mizhael**
- **Role:** The protagonist, initially a simple farmer. Through a twist of fate and obligation to the Legion, Mizhael now wields the powerful Anchor.  
- **Personality:** Reluctant and introspective, Mizhael struggles to reconcile his quiet past with the dangerous responsibilities thrust upon him. Despite his doubts, he demonstrates a strong sense of resolve and adaptability.  
- **Appearance:** A lean, weathered figure, bearing the rough hands of a farmer. The Anchor on his hand stands out as an alien, crystalline object of immense power.  
- **Skills and Traits:**
  - Quick learner under pressure, adapting to the Anchor’s abilities with growing confidence.
  - Keen sense of observation and problem-solving.
  - Untrained in combat but resourceful, relying on the Anchor and Korric for survival.  
- **Motivations:** To survive and discover his own purpose amidst growing chaos.

---

#### **Korric**
- **Role:** Mizhael’s guide and protector, a seasoned warrior loyal to the Legion.  
- **Personality:** Grounded and pragmatic, Korric is focused on survival and loyalty. He offers blunt advice and values practical solutions over lofty ideals. His experience and cynicism balance Mizhael’s uncertainty.  
- **Appearance:** A rugged man in his late 30s or early 40s, scarred from battles. His heavy axe and chainmail armor give him a constant aura of readiness.  
- **Skills and Traits:**
  - Exceptional close-quarters combat skills.
  - Strong tactical sense, providing valuable insights during encounters.
  - Protective instincts, particularly toward Mizhael, though he is careful not to coddle him.  
- **Motivations:** To uphold his duty to the Legion and ensure Mizhael survives long enough to prove useful.  

---

#### **Mask**
- **Role:** The enigmatic leader of the Sundered Legion, whose authority shapes the faction’s actions.  
- **Personality:** Charismatic but shrouded in secrecy. Mask is highly strategic, favoring calculated risks and maintaining control over all operations. Her leadership inspires both loyalty and fear.  
- **Appearance:** Always masked, with no visible features to reveal her identity. Her movements are deliberate, her posture commanding, and her voice sharp but measured.  
- **Skills and Traits:**
  - Master of strategy and espionage.
  - Skilled negotiator, capable of rallying disparate groups under a common goal.
  - Ruthlessly pragmatic, prioritizing the Legion’s survival and success over personal connections.  
- **Motivations:** To consolidate the Legion’s power and ensure its dominance in the region. Mask’s interest in Mizhael and the Anchor suggests she sees them as tools for a larger plan.

---

#### **Eldrin**
- **Role:** A mage working with the Legion, tasked with deciphering ancient artifacts, such as the vial and scroll.  
- **Personality:** Cerebral, cautious, and obsessive. Eldrin’s curiosity about the arcane often overrides his caution, though he remains aware of the risks.  
- **Appearance:** A wiry older man with piercing eyes, his robes faded and fraying from years of use. His workshop is a chaotic mess of scrolls, tomes, and alchemical instruments.  
- **Skills and Traits:**
  - Mastery of ancient languages, runes, and rituals.
  - Alchemical expertise, particularly in analyzing magical substances.
  - Deep knowledge of the region’s history and artifacts, though some regard him as overly focused on academic pursuits.  
- **Motivations:** To uncover the mysteries of the vial and Anchor, balancing academic curiosity with a sense of duty to the Legion.

---

### **Supporting Characters**

#### **Lord Terric**
- **Role:** A local noble indebted to the Legion, approached by Mizhael on Mask’s orders.  
- **Personality:** Suspicious and nervous, likely overwhelmed by the Legion’s influence.  
- **Appearance:** A thin, twitchy man in fine but worn clothing, showing signs of financial trouble.  
- **Motivations:** To repay his debt to the Legion and avoid further trouble.
